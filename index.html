Astraverse

Astraverse is an upcoming Minecraft Java server aimed to provide the best experience for everyone - opening for a closed beta soon.

Our goal is to host a variety of minigames that everyone can enjoy and develop a friendly community. From classic survival Minecraft with no admin intervention, to duelling it out with fun PVP minigames, to escaping fun parkour maps - there's plenty of fun to have!
