Astraverse

Astraverse is an upcoming Minecraft Java server aimed to provide the best experience for everyone - opening for a closed beta soon.

Our goal is to host a variety of things to do that everyone can enjoy, while developing a friendly community. From classic survival Minecraft with no admin intervention, to duelling it out with fun PVP minigames, to escaping fun parkour maps - there's plenty of fun to have!



Watch the teaser










Astra SMP

Astra SMP has undergone a variety of changes - originating from an humble closed Bedrock SMP to becoming a part of Astraverse. This part of Astraverse is just a classic survival world in which you and your friends can join and play in together as you try to survive the dangers that lurk in Minecraft. No mods, no admin intervention.



More information soon










PVP

PVP is one of the four major aspects of Astraverse. We host a multitude of minigames that have been tested for the upmost quality, and built on maps that look visually appeasing to the eye.



More information soon










Parkour

Astraverse's parkour has been specially crafted to be fun and enjoyable while also proiding an unique experience to many. Enjoy highly curated maps while you try and escape. You can also just practice in the practice area, as well as find secrets hidden everywhere!
